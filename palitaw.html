Palitaw

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of glutionous rice

  • 1/4 cup of sugar

  • 1/2 cup of water

  • 1/4 cup of sugar

  • 1/4 cup of toasted sesame seeds

  • 1/4 cup of grated coconut

Procedure:

  1. In a bowl, combine the glutinous rice flour, sugar (if using), and salt.

  2. Once the dough is ready, divide it into small portions (about 1 tablespoon each). Roll each portion into a ball and flatten it with your palms to form a small disc or patty, about 2 inches wide.

  3. Once the palitaw pieces float to the surface, let them cook for an additional 1-2 minutes to ensure they are cooked through.

  4. In a small bowl, mix the sugar, toasted sesame seeds, and grated coconut.

  5. SWhile the palitaw is still warm, roll each piece in the sugar-sesame-coconut mixture until they are well-coated.
