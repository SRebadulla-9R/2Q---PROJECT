Inihaw

Ingredients:

  • 1kg of pork belly

  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce

  • 1 medium onion

  • 4 cloves of garlic

  • 1/4 cup of vinegar

  • 1-2 red chillies

  • 1 tablespoon of sugar

  • Fish sauce

Procedure:

  1. In a bowl, combine soy sauce, vinegar, calamansi or lemon juice, sugar, minced onion, minced garlic, fish sauce (if using), ground black pepper, and red chilies (if using)./p>

  2. Add the pork belly (or pork shoulder) slices into the marinade. Make sure the meat is fully coated with the marinade. For best results, marinate the pork for at least 30 minutes, but if you have more time, marinate it for 1-2 hours or overnight in the refrigerator. The longer you marinate, the more flavorful the pork will be.

  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. If you're using a charcoal grill, make sure the coals are evenly distributed and glowing red. If you're using a gas grill, preheat it to medium-high.

  4. Place the pork on the grill (or the skewers on the rack) and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until the pork is nicely charred and cooked through. Use tongs to turn the meat occasionally to avoid burning.

  5. Serve the Inihaw na Baboy with steamed rice and dipping sauces such as soy sauce with calamansi, suka (vinegar) with chopped onions, or a spicy vinegar dipping sauce.
