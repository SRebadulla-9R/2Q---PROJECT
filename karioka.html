Karioka

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of condensed milk

  • 1 tablespoon butter or margarine

  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

  • Sugar

Procedure:

  1. After the filling cools enough to handle, take small portions and roll them into small balls (about the size of a marble or a walnut). Set them aside on a parchment-lined tray or plate.

  2. Dip each coconut ball into the melted sugar, ensuring it is completely covered.

  3. Cook the Chicken: Add the chicken pieces to the pot and cook until the chicken is lightly browned on all sides, about 5-7 minutes.

  4. Place the coated Karioka on a tray and allow them to set in the refrigerator for about 30 minutes to firm up.
