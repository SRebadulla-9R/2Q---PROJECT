Arroz Caldo

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of glutionous rice

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

  • 1 medium onion

  • 2 cloves of garlic

  • 2-inch piece of ginger

  • 1 lb of chicken

  • 6 cups of chicken broth

  • 2 tablespoons of fish sauce

  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground pepper

  • Salt

  • 2-3 boiled eggs

  • Calamansi

Procedure:

  1. Prepare the Rice: Rinse the glutinous rice under cold water to remove excess starch. Drain and set aside.

  2. Sauté the Aromatics: In a large pot, heat vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onions, minced garlic, and ginger slices. Sauté for about 2-3 minutes, or until the onions become translucent and the ginger is fragrant.

  3. Cook the Chicken: Add the chicken pieces to the pot and cook until the chicken is lightly browned on all sides, about 5-7 minutes.

  4. Add Broth and Rice: Pour in the chicken broth (or water with chicken bouillon). Stir in the rinsed rice, fish sauce (or soy sauce), and ground pepper. Bring to a boil.

  5. Simmer: Lower the heat to medium-low and let the mixture simmer for about 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the rice becomes soft and the soup thickens to a porridge-like consistency. If the porridge gets too thick, you can add more broth or water to adjust the consistency.

  6. Ladle the arroz caldo into bowls. Top with a boiled egg (if using), chopped green onions, and fried garlic. Serve with calamansi or lemon on the side for added zest.
