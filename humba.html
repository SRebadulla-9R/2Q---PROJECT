Humba

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg of pork belly

  • 4 onion

  • 2-3 pieces of dried bay leaves

  • 1/4 cup of vinegar

  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar

  • 1/2 cup of pineapple juice

  • 1/2 pieces star anise

  • 1/2 cup of salted black beans

Procedure:

  1. In a bowl, combine the pork belly pieces, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and bay leaves. Massage the pork with the marinade and let it sit for at least 30 minutes (or up to an hour) to allow the flavors to penetrate the meat.

  2. Add the marinated pork belly to the pot and cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the meat begins to brown and caramelize a bit. This step helps develop the flavor.

  3. Pour in the pineapple juice (if using), water, and add the brown sugar. Stir well to combine, ensuring the sugar dissolves completely.

  4. Add the whole peppercorns, star anise (if using), and a pinch of salt to the pot. Stir everything together.

  5. Once the pork is tender and the sauce has thickened to your liking, remove the pot from the heat.
