Tapsilog

Ingredients:

  • 500g of Tapa

  • 2 cups of cooked rice

  • 3 cloves of garlic

  • Vegetable oil

  • 2 eggs

  • Salt & pepper

Procedure:

  1. Cook the Tapa: Heat the vegetable oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the marinated beef and cook for about 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the beef is tender and browned. Set aside.

  2. Prepare the Garlic Fried Rice: In a large pan or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant and golden brown.

  3. Add the cooked rice, breaking up any clumps, and stir to combine with the garlic. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Fry the rice for 3-5 minutes, occasionally stirring, until it's heated through and slightly crispy. Set aside.

  4. On a plate, place a serving of sinangag (garlic fried rice), top it with the tapa (marinated beef), and add the itlog (fried egg) on the side.

  5. COptionally, you can garnish with sliced tomatoes, cucumber, or a dash of vinegar for added flavor.
