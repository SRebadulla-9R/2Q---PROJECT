Munggo

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of mung beans

  • 4 cups of water

  • 1 medium onion

  • 3 cloves of garlic

  • 1 medium tomato

  • 1 cup of pork

  • 1 bunch of malunggay

  • Fish sauce

Procedure:

  1. In a separate pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and cook until they become translucent.

  2. If using pork or beef, add the meat to the pan and cook until it turns brown. You can also cook the meat first before adding the onions and garlic for extra flavor.

  3. Once the mung beans are tender, add the saut√©ed onion, garlic, and tomato mixture into the pot with the beans. Stir well to combine.

  4. Once everything is cooked and the potatoes (if using) are tender, add the spinach or malunggay leaves. Stir gently and let them cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.

  5. Serve the munggo hot with steamed rice.
