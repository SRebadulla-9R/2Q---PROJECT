Bibingka

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of rice flour

  • 1/2 cup of coconut milk

  • 1/4 cup of sugar

  • 1/4 cup of butter

  • 2 large eggs

  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder

  • 1/2 cup of grated coconut

  • 1/4 cup of water

  • 1/2 a cup of cheese

  • Salted eggs

  • Grated coconut

Procedure:

  1. If you have banana leaves, briefly char them over an open flame or heat them under the broiler to make them pliable. Line your baking pan (about 9x9 inches or a similar size) with the banana leaves. If you don't have banana leaves, you can grease the pan with butter or cooking spray instead.

  2. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, mixing until you get a smooth batter. If the batter feels too thick, you can add a little water to achieve a pourable but thick consistency.

  3. Pour the bibingka batter into the prepared pan, smoothing the top with a spatula.

  4. Bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes or until the bibingka is set and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  5. After 25-30 minutes, remove the bibingka from the oven. Quickly arrange the sliced salted eggs and grated cheese on top of the rice cake. Return the pan to the oven and bake for an additional 5-10 minutes until the cheese melts and the salted eggs are heated through.

  6. Let the bibingka cool slightly before cutting it into squares or wedges. Serve warm as a delicious snack or dessert.
